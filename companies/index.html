How a company is born

01
Identity
A unique ENS name is registered — often based on a date of birth or personal meaning. This becomes the company's address.
02
Assets
Fundamental DeFi protocols are placed on the balance sheet and locked for the maximum available term.
03
Key
Ownership is delivered through a private access setup — from a recovery phrase to a Tangem card or ring, depending on the case.
04
Autonomy
The company begins to work: cash flow starts accruing through the protocols, visible onchain at any time.
