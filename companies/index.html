How a company is born

01
Identity
A unique ENS name is registered — often based on a date of birth or personal meaning. This becomes the company's address.
02
Assets
Assets are selected and placed on the balance sheet of the company being created, based on the chosen strategy.
03
Key
Ownership is delivered through a private access setup — from a recovery phrase to a Tangem card or ring, based on the owner's preference.
04
Autonomy
The company begins to work: cash flow starts accruing through the protocols, visible onchain at any time.
