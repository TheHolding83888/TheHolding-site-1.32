AI Protocol Thesis

Autonomous agent economies require native coordination layers. Virtuals and ElizaOS represent infrastructure for AI-to-AI transactions—not applications, but the rails applications will run on. Position sizing reflects conviction in multi-year adoption curves.

Digital Real Estate Strategy

Metaverse land holdings in Decentraland, Sandbox, and OVR are spatial primitives—scarce coordinates in synthetic realities. As virtual world economies mature, prime locations accrue network effects. TON namespace NFTs follow similar logic: owning @username or premium numbers is positional advantage in Telegram's 900M+ user base.

Portfolio Construction

The best venture investments are protocols that become infrastructure. We hold until adoption is inevitable or thesis breaks.
