Mission & Mandate

Fructus bridges the physical and digital economies by accumulating tokenized real-world assets that generate verifiable cashflow. Real estate, treasuries, commodities, equity in productive businesses—traditional yield-bearing instruments brought onchain with blockchain transparency and 24/7 liquidity.

The fund operates on a simple thesis: the most reliable income comes from assets with intrinsic productivity. Buildings generate rent. Treasuries pay interest. Businesses produce profits. These are not speculative instruments—they are productive assets with centuries of track record, now accessible through digital rails.

Income from the Defitea fund flows directly into Fructus, reinvesting DeFi yields into tangible real-world productivity. Digital income compounds into physical asset ownership. Onchain cashflow fuels offchain income generation.

This creates a virtuous cycle: DeFi protocols generate yield → yield purchases RWA tokens → RWA assets produce real-world income → income compounds portfolio value. Two complementary income streams, one unified holding structure.
