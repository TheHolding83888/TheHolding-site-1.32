Mission & Mandate

Fructus bridges the physical and digital economies by accumulating tokenized real-world assets that generate verifiable cashflow. Real estate, treasuries, commodities, equity in productive businesses—traditional yield-bearing instruments brought onchain with blockchain transparency and 24/7 liquidity.

The fund operates on a simple thesis: the most reliable income comes from assets with intrinsic productivity. Buildings generate rent. Treasuries pay interest. Businesses produce profits. These are not speculative instruments—they are productive assets with centuries of track record, now accessible through digital rails.

Income from the Defitea fund flows directly into Fructus, reinvesting DeFi yields into tangible real-world productivity. Digital income compounds into physical asset ownership. Onchain cashflow fuels offchain income generation.

The future of investing won't be fragmented. It will be tokenized. In an era defined by heightened inflation, geopolitical turbulence, and supply chain disruptions, traditional portfolio construction demands access to hard assets—commodities, energy, industrial materials. Tokenization removes the friction. What once required brokerage accounts, custody arrangements, and geographic limitations now executes onchain with instant settlement and transparent ownership. Fructus constructs a resilient portfolio across essential value stores, combining treasury stability, commodity exposure, and productive equity—all unified through blockchain infrastructure.

This creates a virtuous cycle: DeFi protocols generate yield → yield purchases RWA tokens → RWA assets produce real-world income → income compounds portfolio value. Two complementary income streams, one unified holding structure.

COPXon — Global Copper Miners

Strategic exposure to electrification infrastructure through copper production

COPXon represents tokenized holdings in the Global X Copper Miners ETF, providing targeted access to companies engaged in copper exploration, mining, and refining. Copper stands at the intersection of decarbonization and digitalization—the indispensable conductor powering electric vehicles, renewable energy systems, data centers, and smart infrastructure. As the world electrifies, copper demand accelerates while new supply development faces decade-long permitting timelines and declining ore grades.

This structural supply-demand imbalance creates compelling economics for established producers. The ETF concentrates holdings in large-cap miners with operational scale, geographic diversification, and proven reserve bases—companies positioned to capitalize on multi-decade copper demand growth without the execution risk of greenfield projects.
